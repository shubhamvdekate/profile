Common Questions

Can't find what you're looking for? Just shoot me a message directly.

How quickly can you start on a new project?
Usually within 1–2 weeks depending on my current project load. For urgent requirements, reach out and I'll try to accommodate. I always prefer a proper project onboarding over rushing.
Do you work with startups or only established companies?
Both! I love working with early-stage startups to ship an MVP quickly, and I enjoy scaling products for established teams. Budget and scope tend to differ, but the quality of work remains the same.
What does your development process look like?
Discovery → Architecture & Planning → Iterative Sprints → Testing & QA → Deployment → Post-launch support. I believe in transparent, frequent communication throughout — no black boxes.
Do you provide ongoing maintenance after launch?
Yes! I offer monthly retainer packages for maintenance, bug fixes, and minor feature additions. Many of my clients have been with me for 3+ years post-launch.
Can you work with an existing Android/Flutter codebase?
Absolutely. Code audits, refactoring legacy code, migrating from Java to Kotlin, XML to Compose — I've done it all. I'll start with a thorough review and give you an honest assessment.
