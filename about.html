I'm Shubham Dekate, a Mobile App Developer with 9+ years of experience building Android and Flutter applications that users love. My journey started back in 2015 when I wrote my first Android app — a simple calculator — and got completely hooked on the magic of tapping a screen and seeing code come alive.

Over the years, I've grown from writing Java to mastering Kotlin, from XML layouts to Jetpack Compose, and from single-platform to cross-platform with Flutter and KMM. Each project taught me that great apps aren't just about code — they're about empathy for the user, obsession with performance, and a commitment to clean architecture.

Today I specialize in complex Android applications, scalable Flutter products, and backend APIs powered by Ktor. I've shipped apps downloaded by millions, led engineering teams, and mentored developers early in their careers. When I'm not coding, I contribute to open source, write technical articles, and explore the intersection of mobile and emerging tech.

My philosophy: // clean code is a love letter to your future self.

// what drives me

My Philosophy

Performance First
Every millisecond matters. I obsess over cold start times, smooth 60fps animations, and minimal battery drain.
Clean Architecture
Separation of concerns, SOLID principles, and testable code. Built to evolve, not to rewrite.
User Empathy
I build for humans. Accessibility, intuitive UX, and delight in the small details make apps people love.
Ship & Iterate
Perfect is the enemy of shipped. I believe in small, frequent releases backed by analytics and user feedback.
